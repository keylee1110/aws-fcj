# Reflection Report: “Building Agentic AI & Context Optimization”
&emsp;**Event:** Agentic AI Workshop.
&emsp;**Date:** Friday, December 5, 2025.
&emsp;**Time:** 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM.
&emsp;**Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Tower, Ho Chi Minh City.

### Purpose of the Event.
- Explore the concept of "Agentic AI" - AI that can take action.
- Learn techniques for context optimization in LLMs.

### Key Contents.
- **Agents for Amazon Bedrock:** How to define agents and action groups.
- **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation):** Enhancing model responses with external data.
- **Context Window Management:** Strategies for handling long conversations.

### Key Takeaways.
- Agents allow LLMs to interact with internal systems and APIs.
- RAG is essential for keeping AI responses up-to-date and accurate.

### Event Experience.
- Fascinating to see AI performing complex multi-step tasks.
- The demo on "Travel Booking Agent" was very impressive.
