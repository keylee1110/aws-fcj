# Reflection Report: “AWS Cloud Day Vietnam 2025 & GenAI Track”
&emsp;**Event:** AWS Vietnam Cloud Day.

&emsp;**Date:** Thursday, September 18, 2025.

&emsp;**Time:** 9:00 AM – 17:00 PM (GMT+7).

&emsp;**Location:** AWS Vietnam Office, Bitexco Financial Tower, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City.


### Purpose of the Event.

   - Introduce the latest technology trends from AWS, especially Generative AI.
   - Share case studies from major enterprises in Vietnam and across the region.
   - Discuss leadership strategies and organizational management in the AI era.
   - Explore best practices for security and real-world applications of AI Agents.

### Key Contents.

#### Part 1: Main Sessions (AWS Cloud Day Vietnam).

- **Opening & Keynote:** Overview of AWS’s vision in Vietnam and Southeast Asia.
- **Customer Keynotes:** Applying AI in finance and blockchain sectors.
- **AWS Keynote:** Regional tech trends and strategies to empower innovation.

#### Part 2: GenAI Track.

- **Building a Unified Data Foundation:** Strategies for scalable AI & analytics.
- **GenAI Adoption Roadmap:** Vision and services supporting enterprise adoption.
- **AI-Driven Development Lifecycle (AI-DLC):** Integrating AI into software development.
- **Securing GenAI Applications:** Addressing security challenges in infrastructure and models.
- **Beyond Automation - AI Agents:** AI Agents as intelligent partners driving productivity.

### Key Takeaways.

- GenAI is a catalyst for organizational transformation, not just a tool.
- Security is fundamental and must be integrated from the ground up (Zero Trust).
- AI Agents represent the next leap in automation and productivity.

### Event Experience.

- **Networking:** Met with AWS experts and industry leaders.
- **Learning:** Gained deep insights into GenAI security and architecture.
- **Atmosphere:** Professional, energetic, and inspiring.
