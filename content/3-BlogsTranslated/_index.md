### AWS Tech U Program Survival Guide

This blog introduces AWS Tech U - a career acceleration program for early career talent, combining technical training and soft skills (ProSkills). It shares strategies for conquering AWS certifications from foundational to advanced levels, how to expand knowledge through Technical Field Communities (TFCs) and Shadowing activities. Finally, it outlines 4 tips for success: set high expectations, find teammates, own your schedule, and accumulate knowledge every day.

### Enhance contact center workforce management with new Amazon Connect Forecasting, Capacity Planning, and Scheduling features (Q2 2025)

This article introduces new Amazon Connect features that optimize workforce management through AI. Key highlights include: Customizing work activities to track detailed tasks; Editing forecasts directly on the UI for quick reaction to changes; and Setting recurring activities to automate scheduling. These features help reduce operational costs and increase forecast accuracy without the need for third-party tools.

### Best performance and fastest memory with new Amazon EC2 R8i and R8i-flex instances

AWS launches the 8th generation memory-optimized instances (R8i and R8i-flex) powered by custom Intel Xeon 6 processors. The R8i series delivers 15% better price performance and 20% higher overall performance compared to the previous generation, suitable for mission-critical workloads like Databases or Big Data. The R8i-flex version offers cost optimization (5% cheaper) for workloads that do not fully utilize compute resources. Both support the fastest memory bandwidth in the cloud.
